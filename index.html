About

Rongwu Xu is currently a PhD student at the Allen School of CSE, University of Washington. Born and raised in Beijing, he completed both his undergrad and Master's education in Computer Science at Tsinghua University. Much of his work is around AI alignment and human-AI interaction, which he is passionate about because it bridges the gap between machines and humans. He has broad research interests across NLP, AI, psychology, and philosophy.
