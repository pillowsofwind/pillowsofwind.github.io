Short Biography [中文]

Rongwu Xu is currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Washington. He received his Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Tsinghua University in 2022 and 2025, respectively. His research interests include artificial intelligence and natural language processing. He has published multiple papers at top-tier international conferences such as ACL, EMNLP, ICLR, and NeurIPS. He has received the ACL Outstanding Paper Award, the Tsinghua University Outstanding Graduate Award, the Outstanding Master's Thesis Award, the National Scholarship, and several other university-level honors from Tsinghua University.

许融武现为华盛顿大学博士生。于2022年和2025年先后获得清华大学学士和硕士学位，研究方向为人工智能与自然语言处理。他在 ACL、EMNLP、ICLR、NeurIPS 等国际顶级会议上发表多篇论文。他曾获得 ACL 杰出论文奖、清华大学优秀毕业生，优秀硕士毕业论文，国家奖学金及清华大学多项校级荣誉。
