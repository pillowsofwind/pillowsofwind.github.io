Rongwu Xu (许 融武)

I am a second year MS student in computer science at IIIS@Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Wei Xu.

My research interests span security&privacy and deep learning, as well as their intersections. Recently, I have been focusing on studying the safety, security, and privacy issues in large language models.

Previously, I explored topics on real-world private computing systems. I have built secure systems that can be used in authentication and deep learning.

Prior to graduate school, I obtained my bachelor degree in computer science from CST@Tsinghua University in 2022.
