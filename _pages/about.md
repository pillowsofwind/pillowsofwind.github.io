This is the front page of pillowsofwind's personal website. The name *pillowsofwind* comes from band [Pink Floyd](https://www.pinkfloyd.com/home.php)'s song '*A Pillow of Wind*s' from the Album Meddle, November 11, 1971. 

# Short bio

Rongwu Xu is a first year MS student in Computer Science at [IIIS, Tsinghua Univ.](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), advised by [Prof. Zhixuan Fang](https://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~fang/). He is also given guidance from [Prof. Fan Zhang](https://www.fanzhang.me). He feels very fortunate to learn from both advisors. He has a broad research interest in computer security and privacy, as well as more general system topics. Recently, he focuses on blockchain and DeFi areas. Before graduate school, he obtained BE in Computer Science from [Dept. of Computer Science and Tech., Tsinghua Univ.](https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/).
