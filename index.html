I am a master student in computer science at IIIS@Tsinghua University. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor degree in computer science from CST@Tsinghua University.

My research interests are: natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLMs).
Currently, I mainly focus on:

(1) Data-centric learning;
(2) Evaluation & simulation: dataset, metric and framework;
(3) Societal impact and safety.
