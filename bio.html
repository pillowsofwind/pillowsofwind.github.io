Short Biography [中文]

Rongwu Xu, male, born in 2000, Beijing. He holds a Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University, and is currently pursuing his Master's degree, also at Tsinghua University. His research interests include artificial intelligence and natural language processing. He has published multiple papers at conferences including ACL/EMNLP/ICLR/NeurIPS. He has received an ACL Outstanding Paper Award, National Scholarship from the Ministry of Education of China and multiple accolades from Tsinghua University.

许融武，男，2000年生，北京市人。2022年获清华大学学士学位，现攻读清华大学硕士学位。他的研究方向为人工智能、自然语言处理。他在包括 ACL/EMNLP/ICLR/NeurIPS 等国际会议上发表了多篇论文。他曾获得ACL杰出论文奖，国家奖学金和多个清华大学校级荣誉奖项。
